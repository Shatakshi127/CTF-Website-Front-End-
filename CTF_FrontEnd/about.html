The Clown Canard?
ACP Darsh Kalra is a youthful, motivated, and promising individual. The 30 year-long Great Royal Circus Festival is about to held again. One of the most recognisable and enduring centres of fascination for everyone was the Rambo Circus. But destiny has its own scheme. A terrible short circuit forced the closure of the circus. Unfortunately, this episode came to light 30 years later and is now widely shared on social media. As a result, the matter is widely demanded to be reopened by the public. The case has been given to ACP Darsh Kalra. He discovers the old case file from thirty years ago, which contains the following items:
  • Vintage newspaper clipping
  • Vintage negatives of some images
  • Journal of the former clown
