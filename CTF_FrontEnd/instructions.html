INSTRUCTIONS 

Now that you are a part of our community, you must know of some rules we follow around here.

General Rules and Instructions
  • The Clown Canard is an exciting Capture the Flag event with a time duration of three hours
  • It is a team event consisting of three participants each.
  • Each team must register on the designated portal (Colloquim Website), and every team member will have access to a single account.
  • Initially, each team will get 500 points to unlock the questions.
  • Ranks can be viewed on the leaderboard page. The leaderboard time is updated everytime a submission is made.
  • There will be three levels having four questions each. At each level, the team members must solve these four questions in any order and connect all the dots from each level to conclude the final answer. There is only one correct answer to a question.
  • Unlocking and winning points for a question will change dynamically depending on the number of teams solving that question. Participants will be able to see changes directly on their laptop screens.
  • Each question will have a minimum unlock point irrespective of dynamic change.
  • Participants will get enough clues to solve the questions. Using clues reduces some team points.
  • At each level, the team can skip any one question on the condition of getting 50% of their points deducted for that particular question.
  • Participants may ask moderators whether they are on the right track (Only Yes or No).
  • Your method of solving clues may be verified to ensure a fair evaluation. Participants have to explain how they arrived at the final answer.
  • Participants will bring their laptops fully charged with a proper internet connection.
  • The team with the correct answer will be the winner, and in case of a tie, points and time will be the deciding factor.
Special Rules and Instructions
  • Dont try to hack anything apart from this CTF Network
  • Dont try to hack other members
  • Dont spoil! Dont share how you solved each challenge with other members.
  • Performing denial of service attacks on the server will lead to disqualification. You are requested to play ethically.
  • Brute force attacks on the flag form is prohibited.
  • Remember, once the timer starts, it can't be paused. The timer will not resume if you logout and log back in.
